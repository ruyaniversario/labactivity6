    • Arduino based insect tracker
    Tags: Arduino, tracker
    Author: Chantal Reyes, Nichole Caluag, Robniel Diaz
    Published: November 15, 2020. 9:39 a.m by admin
    Adviser: Dosado, Roy Cyril
    Abstract

    Mosquitoes and other insects are harmful for humans as they can cause many diseases which are harmful for human health. In order to avoid the mosquitoes, people use chemical repellents which affect the environment adversely or mosquito repeller skin creams can create skin problems. This paper proposes the smart ultrasonic insect repelling system driven by harvesting energy from solar panels. The ultrasonic waves have frequencies more than 20,000 Hz which are inaudible to humans but when insects come in the contact with ultrasonic waves, they sense the waves from special hair or sensilla present on the antennae of mosquitoes which will create stress on the nervous system and jam their own ultrasonic frequency and force them to leave that area.
